In this guide you will learn how to buy/sell goods on our website. For better understanding visit the personal guide for customers and retailers.

Note:
Here, on this website no deals or transactions will occur. Our aim is to connect customers and retailers.








Guide for Retailers:

If you are a retailer and is willing to sell your product, then you are on the right place. Here, you can put your product online and sell it offline. The process is pretty simple. You need to go to settings and sign in. Then, it will ask your address and other details. You need to fill it appropriately so that when customers will want to buy your product they can get the right data. If you have any queries visit the Contact Us section.






Guide for Customers:

If you are a customer and is willing to buy a product, then you are on the right place. Here, you can check a product online and buy it offline. The process is pretty simple. You need to go to settings and sign in. Then, it will ask your details. You need to fill it appropriately so that retailers can keep the product ready for you and contact you if needed. If you have any queries visit the Contact Us section.
